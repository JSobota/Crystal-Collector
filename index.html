Crystal Collector

How To Play

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to you total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes about the random number.

Each time the game starts, the values of the crystals will change.
